Event Creation Guidelines
Best Practices:
  • Use clear, descriptive titles
  • Provide comprehensive descriptions
  • Include accurate dates and times
  • Link relevant participants
  • Add covering journalists when known
  • Be objective and factual
Avoid:
  • Vague or misleading titles
  • Unverified information
  • Biased or inflammatory language
  • Incorrect dates or details
  • Duplicate events
  • Personal opinions as facts
Types of Events to Document:
Political
Protests, elections, government actions
Social
Community gatherings, cultural events
Conflict
Military actions, clashes, incidents
Humanitarian
Aid distribution, rescue operations
Economic
Market changes, business events
Other
Natural disasters, accidents
Review Process: All events are subject to review before being approved. Events will be marked as "Pending" until reviewed by administrators. Please ensure all information is accurate and well-documented.
