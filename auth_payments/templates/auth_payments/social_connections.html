Social Connection Help

  1. Click the "Connect" button next to the social provider you want to use
  2. You'll be redirected to the provider's login page
  3. Sign in with your social account credentials
  4. Authorize our application to access your basic profile information
  5. You'll be redirected back and the account will be connected

We only access basic profile information including:
  • Your name and profile picture
  • Your email address (if public)
  • Your unique user ID from the provider
We do not access your posts, friends list, or other private information.

  1. Find the connected account in the "Connected Accounts" section above
  2. Click the "Disconnect" button next to the account
  3. Confirm the disconnection in the popup dialog
  4. The account will be immediately disconnected
Note: Make sure you have another way to sign in (email/password or another connected account) before disconnecting.
